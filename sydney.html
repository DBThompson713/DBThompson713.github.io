A picture book I saw in grade 2 changed my life.

I was born and raised in Northern Manitoba, Canada.

In grade two, we had to pick a country out of a big picture book of countries and write a small report on a major city.

My turn came, I flipped through the book.

Then I saw a strange building unlike anything I had ever seen before.

I was in awe. Over the next couple pages I was treated to photos of this city and it's people

I knew then that I would one day go to Sydney.

It took two decades for me to set foot on Australian soil.

I excitedly asked the customs agent whether "Border Security" was still filming. She seemed amused by the excited Canadian couple and wished us luck.

That was 5 years ago. I am now an Australian permanent resident.
